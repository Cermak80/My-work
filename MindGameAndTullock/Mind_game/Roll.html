Part 1

In this experiment, you will be matched with another participant, whom we will call your opponent.

First, please think of any two numbers from 1 to 6 and remember them.

At the end of these instructions we will ask you to roll a die using the online app Roll a Die. You will need to write the number you roll into the space provided below.

There are two possible outcomes of the game:

  • If the number you roll is one of the two numbers you thought of, please select “Yes” at the bottom of the form. Your payment will be £2, and your opponent will be paid £0.
  • If the number you roll is not one of the two numbers you thought of, please mark “No” at the bottom of the form. Your payoff will be £0, and your opponent will be paid £2.
